The 7-Day Plan

DAY 1
Deep Learning Fundamentals
Backprop from scratch, normalization & residuals, optimizers, and a training-debugging playbook.
DAY 2
Transformers & LLMs
Attention derivation, KV cache, scaling laws, and the SFT→RLHF→DPO post-training landscape.
DAY 3
Data + Evaluation & Benchmarking
Data as an experimental variable, contamination detection, ablations, and the eval-design framework.
DAY 4
Multimodal & Agentic AI
CLIP/ViT, audio/video, agents & tool use, RAG — plus a 65-question full mock drill.
DAY 5
Research Taste + Paper Deep-Dives
The weak→strong answer ladder, 5-point paper frame, anchor papers (Transformer, Chinchilla, FlashAttention, DPO, GRPO/RLVR, CLIP), the believe-it checklist, and 14 drills.
DAY 6
Post-Training: Sampling & RL
Decoding (temperature, top-p, speculative, structured) and the full policy-gradient → PPO → DPO → GRPO/RLVR lineage, with derivations and 14 mock drills.
DAY 7
100 Drills (Days 1–6)
One ruthless final pass — 100 self-contained rapid-fire Q&As across DL fundamentals, transformers, data/eval, multimodal/agents, taste, sampling & RL, and systems.
